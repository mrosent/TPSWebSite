The TrustPoint Story

TrustPoint was born from a simple observation: law firm websites often feel unwelcoming, causing potential clients to stay silent or abandon contact attempts. Meanwhile, attorneys spend countless hours on repetitive intake calls that drain time and resources.

Julie Lennon built her reputation in personal injury law by making skeptical clients feel heard, understood, and confident. Partnering with technologist Joshua Rain, who specializes in AI-driven video and software, they bring visual storytelling and emotional clarity to legal services at scale.

Jessica Robbins joined the team to ensure that every client partnership thrives, focusing on strategic partnerships and scalable growth strategies. Today, TrustPoint delivers fast, AI-powered FAQ and onboarding videos that convert and build trust that leads to signed cases.
