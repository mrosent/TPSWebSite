TrustPoint Solutions – Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our systems, process, and what to expect when working with TrustPoint.

1. What exactly does TrustPoint Solutions do?

TrustPoint helps law firms grow by building client trust first, then converting that trust into calls, clients, and consistent growth.

We start with attorney-recorded FAQ videos that answer the questions real clients are already asking. Then we deploy a full growth system — SEO, Google visibility, intake automation, CRM, chatbots, and ads — so that trust turns into measurable results without adding work to your plate.

2. Who is TrustPoint best suited for?

TrustPoint is designed for trust-dependent practices, especially:

  • Personal Injury
  • Criminal Defense
  • Family Law
  • Immigration
  • Employment
  • Estate Planning
  • Business & Civil Litigation

We work best with firms that:

  • Want better clients, not just more leads
  • Can’t rely solely on referrals
  • Value ethical, long-term growth over gimmicks
3. How is TrustPoint different from a traditional marketing agency?

Most agencies focus on traffic, ads, or rankings. TrustPoint focuses on trust and conversion.

  • Help clients choose you before they call
  • Automate intake so no opportunities are missed
  • Show you exactly what’s working and why

Video is the entry point. Systems do the heavy lifting.

4. Why does TrustPoint focus so much on video?

Because clients hire the attorney they trust — and nothing builds trust faster than seeing and hearing you answer their real concerns.

  • Pre-qualify clients before they contact you
  • Reduce time spent repeating the same explanations
  • Improve SEO, engagement, and conversion rates

These aren’t commercials. They’re clarity tools.

5. I’m not comfortable on camera. Is this still a fit?

Yes — and this is common.

  • Structured and guided (no improvising)
  • Short and conversational
  • Focused on clarity, not performance

Most attorneys find the process far easier than expected — and the videos continue working long after they’re recorded.

6. Do you guarantee results?

No — and ethically, no legal marketing provider should.

  • Proven systems
  • Transparent metrics
  • Clear expectations
  • Continuous optimization

Our goal is predictable, sustainable growth — not short-term promises.

7. How long does it take to see results?

Timelines vary based on practice area, market, and starting point, but generally:

  • Immediate: Improved intake, faster response times, better client quality
  • 30–90 days: SEO traction, higher conversion rates, better attribution
  • 3–6 months: Consistent inbound flow supported by the Rain Maker System

We prioritize momentum early, then scale responsibly.

8. What is the “Rain Maker System”?

The Rain Maker System is TrustPoint’s full growth engine. It connects:

  • Video content
  • SEO and Google visibility
  • Paid ads (when appropriate)
  • CRM and pipelines
  • AI chatbots and intake automation
  • Attribution and reporting

Everything works together — so nothing falls through the cracks.

9. Will this integrate with our existing systems?

In most cases, yes. TrustPoint can:

  • Replace outdated tools
  • Integrate with existing CRMs and calendars
  • Centralize marketing, intake, and reporting

During onboarding, we map your current workflow and simplify it — not complicate it.

10. Do you handle intake and follow-up automation?

Yes. We build systems that:

  • Respond instantly to new leads
  • Route inquiries correctly
  • Reduce missed calls and no-shows
  • Capture attribution and source data

Your team stays focused on cases — not chasing leads.

11. Is this compliant with legal advertising rules?

Yes. TrustPoint is built specifically for law firms and emphasizes:

  • Ethical messaging
  • Clear disclaimers
  • No outcome guarantees
  • Client-focused education

We help you build trust without crossing ethical lines.

12. Do we own our content and data?

Yes — 100%.

  • You own your videos
  • You own your leads
  • You own your data

TrustPoint builds systems for your firm, not dependencies on us.

13. What does TrustPoint cost?

Pricing depends on:

  • Practice area
  • Market competitiveness
  • Services selected

Most firms start with:

  • A Video + System foundation
  • Then scale into the Rain Maker System as results compound

We provide clear starting points during your demo — no surprises.

14. What’s the first step to getting started?

The first step is a strategy demo, not a sales pitch. We’ll:

  • Review your current marketing and intake flow
  • Identify gaps and missed opportunities
  • Show how TrustPoint would work specifically for your firm

If it’s not a fit, we’ll tell you.
